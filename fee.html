Our Tuition Fee Structure

Flexible, and Accessible Education for All

Our Fee Structure is Based on Two Options:

1. Pure online studies where your child joins live classes online via our online platform, with full access to recorded lessons 24/7.

2. Attending one of our physical locations where your child is supported by our staff to join lessons.

Years 1 – 6

  • Admission fee – £100 (non-refundable)
  • Annual fees – £2500 (Online)

  • Annual fees – £4000 (Attended in one of our physical locations in Ghana)

  • Annual fees – £4800 (Attended in one of our physical locations in the UK)

    • Deposit – £500

    Years 7 - 9

  • Admission fee – £100 (non-refundable)
  • Annual fees – £2400 (Online)

  • Annual fees – £4200 (Attended in one of our physical locations in Ghana)

  • Annual fees – £4800 (Attended in one of our physical locations in the UK)

    • Deposit – £500

    Years 10 - 11

  • Admission fee – £100 (non-refundable)
  • £400 per subject (online)

    • Deposit – £500
